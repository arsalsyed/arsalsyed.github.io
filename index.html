Arsal Syed

I am a final year PhD candidate in Department of Electrical Engineering at University of Nevada Las Vegas, advised by Dr Brendan Morris. My research focuses on building machine/ deep learning algorithms for pedestrain trajectory prediciton.

More specifically I leverage computer vision techniques (CNN, semantic segmentation etc) to understand the influence of scene semantics on pedestrain motion. Recently I have been working on Graph NN and Transformer networks to further improve the prediction performance.

Prior to my PhD I worked in the controls and automation industry for 1.5 years.
